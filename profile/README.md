## ðŸ¤– Projects

Here are some of the diverse and innovative projects developed by our members:

- **IITD Bus Tracker**: A real-time GPS tracking system for campus buses, providing students and staff with accurate arrival times and live locations.
- **Biometric attendance system**: A fingerprint-based system designed for secure, automated attendance in academic settings.
- **Quadruped Robot**: ROS2 Humble-based, IoT-enabled robot featuring advanced locomotion and robust mechatronic design, suitable for dynamic terrains.
- **Hexabot**: A six-legged spider-like robot capable of complex locomotion and stability control using inverse kinematics and adaptive gait algorithms.
- **IoT-Enabled 2D Plotter**: Converts digital sketches into precise pen movements using wireless control.
- **Ball Balancing Bot**: A 3-RRS parallel manipulator that uses a resistive touchpad, PID control, and inverse kinematics to balance a ball.
- **Custom Quadrotor Drone**: A fully self-built drone with in-house motor drivers and remote controller.
- **Pixhawk Drone**: Quadcopter featuring auto takeoff, landing, and mission planning using the Pixhawk flight controller.
